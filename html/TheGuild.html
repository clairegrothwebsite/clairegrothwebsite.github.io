Mae Estriden | Eladrin | Bard

  • Adopted Princess of the Ehoral Kingdom located in the Country Otrar
  • Eladrins are seasonal elves who can change their appearance depending on the season they wish to embody. Mae is an Autumn Eladrin and has been since her adoption at age 6. Her skin is a light red/orange, and she has auburn hair with tree-like antlers.
  • She took up playing the viol at a young age and continued to perform throughout her royal duties
  • Unaware of her birth parents, she left the Ehoral Kingdom to search for any sign of her unknown past and if her birth family is alive

Timber | Red Fox Companion | Rogue

  • Mae's childhood pet, which she received from the High Elf Queen and King who adopted her.
  • A 3-ft-tall Red Fox who excels at stealth and has a keen sense of smell and acute hearing.

Liz Valdivian | Half-Elf | Wizard

  • A Sage whose family owns one of the oldest Elven libraries in the country of Floura. This library has been in the family for generations of Wood Elves on her mother's side. It's nestled deep in the woods and holds many ancient scripts from all cultures.
  • Her mother is a Wood Elf librarian, while her father is a Human carpenter.
  • She has worked and lived in the family library all her life, gathering new texts and learning from the shelves. At the age of 18, she finally left the library in search of new texts to add to her family's collection.
  • At her departure, her father gave her a wooden cabinet to carry these texts. It is worn like a large backpack, almost as tall as Liz herself.

Onyx | Kenku | Ranger

  • Outlander to his hometown in Floura. He was taken in by an old hermit when the town cast him out. He was taught how to live in the deep forests that borders his old home and how to communicate with others.
  • Kenku's are large, raven-like, birds that aren't able to talk on their own accord. Kenku can never produce their own sounds. Their voices were taken by a deity long ago, forcing them to mimic others' words instead.
  • After the old hermit passed, Onyx wielded two sickles and uses his keen knowledge of the land to keep undead monsters at bay. He has made it his mission to protect his old town from the dark that lingers in the forest. His main enemy: his hunger for revenge.

Luna Adhafera | Air Genasi | Cleric

  • An Acolyte who traveled across Savand to spread the word of their deity Celestain: The God of the Stars.
  • As an Air Genasi, she has the elemental power of air and skin painted as night. Her hair is stark white and braided into a ceremonial headpiece. Her voice has the quality of a breeze, airy and quiet.
  • Her family is devoted to following Celestian. Although, she has been away from Findlaire for years, and many things can change.

Irnek Darish | Dragonborn | Sorcerer

  • Former slave to the Drow who took his home country of Shonia by force. While enslaved he was forced to be a record keeper and head general for the Drow forces of slaves. His draconic ability to wield magic was suppressed by specially crafted magical restraints.
  • Dragonborns are a race of humanoid dragons, although they don't have wings. Irnek is a brass dragonborn with muted yellow scales covered in scars from past punishments.
  • Irnek is originally from a small village in Shonia, a barren country home to few kingdoms. War has since destroyed his home and family, leaving him nothing left in Shonia, and few are left to defy the Drows.
  • After escaping captivity, he developed a strong sense of justice and refuses to leave others to the same fate. Though his past still haunts him, he seeks revenge for himself and his country.
